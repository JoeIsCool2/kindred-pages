Privacy

Kindred Pages is designed for sensitive family information. Memorial pages should default to private or invite-only unless a family chooses otherwise.

Family control

Families control page visibility, guest memories, photos, service details, archives, and co-admin access.

Guest memories

Guest submissions are intended to be reviewed by family admins before appearing publicly.

Portability

Families should be able to download an archive before closing or transferring a page.
